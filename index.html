We've got what you need!

Manchester Sound Collective provides an opportunity for the public to enjoy music outside of the mainstream. We run events showcasing the finest unconventional musicians, along with some of Manchester's best food and drink.

As a social enterprise we are committed to helping emerging musicians showcase their talent and provideing the community access to diverse and wonderful music.

Initially our passion for classical music and our craving for live performances was one of the sparks for Manchester Sound Collective's inception. We wanted to eat, drink and talk while we do so. We also recognised the veed for burgeoning musicians more opportunities to perform, so now we offer the chance for up and coming musicians a place to exhibit their talent and gain experience performing in front of a live audience.

We offer a platform to bridge the gap between enjoying listening to music and practicing at home, and the more formal, traditional avenue of a theatre performance. We now offer access to a wide range of music performances (not just classical), from local acts and further afield, from those just starting out and from more seasoned performers. Along with this we provide excellent food made by our own chef, specifically tailored to any venue.

How we can work with..

Venues

We are always seeking to work with locations that can provide an alternative backdrip to our unique events. If you are interested in engaging your local community with your space in a different way, please get in touch. Let us do the work for you.

Individuals

Are you planning a private event or party? Let us do the legwork and bring you live music, excellent food and a unique experience to liven your proceedings. Whatever they might be.

Musicians

We put on all types on music and welcome all performers. Are you looking for an opportunity to share your work with a wide and varied audience? Whether you are an individual or a large-scale ensemble, this could be the perfect platform for your emerging talent.

Events / community areas

Your community group or arts event could work in tandem with our vision of bringing the extraordinary to the widest audience. If you are interested in working with us or would like us to add to your programme get in touch.
